About MediPulse Pharmaceuticals

MediPulse Pharmaceuticals is dedicated to providing safe, affordable medications and trusted health advice to the communities of Harare and Bulawayo. Our pharmacists are licensed and ready to assist with prescriptions, chronic-care counseling and vaccinations where available.

Our Mission

Deliver compassionate pharmacy care and make essential medicines accessible to all.

Our Team

Experienced pharmacists and trained staff focused on patient safety and service.

Certifications

Registered with the national pharmacy council — we follow best practice and storage guidelines.
